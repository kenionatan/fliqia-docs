**Last updated:** February 2026

### Introduction

Fliqia ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Fliqia mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

### Information we collect

**Photos and images:** When you use features such as "Create Visual Story" or filters, you may select photos from your device. These images are processed to provide the requested functionality. We do not store your photos on our servers beyond what is necessary to fulfill your request.

**Device information:** We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to ensure compatibility and improve our services.

**Usage data:** We may collect anonymous usage analytics (e.g., feature usage, crash reports) to improve the app.

### How we use your information

- To provide and maintain the app's features
- To process your images when you use AI-powered features
- To improve our services and fix bugs
- To communicate with you about updates or support, if you contact us

### Third-party services

Our app may use third-party services for:

- **AI and image processing:** When you use AI features, your images may be sent to third-party providers (e.g., cloud-based AI services) to generate results. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle data.

- **Analytics:** We may use analytics providers to understand app usage. Data is typically anonymized.

### Data storage and retention

We do not retain your photos on our servers after processing. Any temporary data used for a request is deleted once the request is complete.

### Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data

Contact us using the information in the [Contact](#contact) section to exercise these rights.

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.
